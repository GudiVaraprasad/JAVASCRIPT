What does that comment means ?

This tool will help you to calculate your BMI index. The BMI Value indicates as follows....

  • Value <18.6, you are UNDERWEIGHT, you need to take some nutrious food and put on weight.
  • Value lies between >18.6 and <26, you are HEALTHY, continue the same.
  • Value >26, you are OVERWEIGHT, you need to exercise yourself.
